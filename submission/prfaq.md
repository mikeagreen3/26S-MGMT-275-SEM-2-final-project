### Schlage Launches AI Access Concierge, an Operating Layer for Short-Term Rental Hosts

*Software update to Schlage Encode and Encode Plus closes every loop in the host's access workflow, from automated guest codes to a pre-arrival check that stops lockouts before they reach the door*

**PALO ALTO, Calif., April 30, 2026.** Allegion today launched Schlage AI Access Concierge, a software update for Schlage Encode and Encode Plus smart locks that handles every step of front-door access for short-term rental hosts. Booking sync, natural-language code scheduling, a pre-arrival health check, and AI-guided recovery now run inside the Schlage Home app at no additional cost.

More than 2 million short-term rental hosts in the United States manage front-door access for guests they will never meet, on platforms (Airbnb, VRBO) that pay them only when the guest gets inside. A lock failure at check-in is the most damaging event in their day. It triggers one-star reviews, host penalties, and emergency locksmith calls that run into the hundreds of dollars. The majority of those failures, dead batteries, Wi-Fi drops, and unsynced codes, are detectable hours in advance. Until today, no one was watching.

"Hosts have told us the same thing for years. They had no idea anything was wrong until the guest called," said Maria Chen, Senior Vice President of Connected Hardware at Allegion. "Schlage sees the most complete picture of lock health in the industry. We built AI Access Concierge to put that data to work for hosts, not leave it sitting in a server log."

The product covers four host jobs. Booking sync connects Airbnb and VRBO so that access codes are created when a reservation is confirmed and revoked at checkout, with no host action required. Natural-language scheduling lets hosts type instructions like "give the cleaning crew access from 11am to 3pm every Saturday in June" and turns them into time-bounded codes automatically. A pre-arrival health check runs two hours before every check-in, scoring each lock across battery level, network connectivity, and code-sync confirmation, then alerting the host with a one-tap fix path when a risk pattern shows up. And when something does fail, an AI diagnoses whether the problem is the lock, the network, or the sync, and gives the host a guided recovery, not a generic error.

AI Access Concierge is available today inside the Schlage Home app for all Schlage Encode and Encode Plus owners.

### Resolved decisions (closed v2 open questions)

1. **Hero feature:** workflow story leads, pre-arrival check is the payoff. Justified by RICE (`../appendix-working-notes/rice-prioritization.md`).
2. **Booking sync mentioned** as job 1 of 4, given its highest-leverage status in the product brief.
3. **Audience tension:** PR commits fully to the host story. The host segment is small but operationally distinct; mixing the broader base would weaken positioning. The non-host majority is not harmed (features stay opt-in inside the app).
4. **Market size:** "More than 2 million short-term rental hosts in the United States." Conservative US-only number, defensible against Airbnb 10-K data. Strategy doc's 5M+ is the global figure, not used in the PR.
5. **Exec quote:** Maria Chen, SVP of Connected Hardware, Allegion (fictional, plausible).
6. **Pricing line removed.** No charge for the feature in the PR. Tiering question lives in the Internal FAQ where pricing strategy belongs.
